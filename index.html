About GOD OF WAR


The God of War series is a critically acclaimed action-adventure franchise developed by Sony’s Santa Monica Studio!


Greek Mythology Era


God of War (2005): The series debuted on the PlayStation,introducing Kratos, a Spartan warrior on a quest for revenge against Ares, the Greek God of War1
. God of War II (2007): Continuing Kratos’ story, this sequel sees him battling the Olympian gods.
God of War: Chains of Olympus (2008): A prequel for the PlayStation Portable, exploring Kratos’ servitude to the gods1.
God of War III (2010): Concluding the original trilogy, Kratos seeks to destroy Zeus and the Olympian pantheon.
God of War: Ghost of Sparta (2010): Another PSP title, delving into Kratos’ past and his relationship with his brother Deimos

Norse Mythology Era


God of War (2018): A soft reboot set in Norse mythology, featuring an older Kratos and his son Atreus. This game explores themes of fatherhood and redemption.
God of War Ragnarök (2022): The latest installment continues Kratos and Atreus’ journey, delving deeper into Norse mythology and the impending apocalypse.
